## Biography 
H. Wu received the Ph.D. degrees from University of Groningen, Groningen, The Netherlands and Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China. He is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow with Hong Kong Centre for Logistics Robotics, CUHK T Stone Robotics Institute, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China. Dr. Wu was a recipient of the Best Paper Award in the 15th IEEE International Conference on Control and Automation 2019. 

## Research Interests 

- Visual servoing
- Adaptive control
- Distributed control
- Observer design
