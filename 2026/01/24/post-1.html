The Manipulation Group at the Hong Kong Centre for Logistics Robotics (HKCLR) is recruiting highly motivated students and researchers to join our team.

Our group works at the intersection of fundamental robotics research and real-world robotic products, with a strong focus on developing robust, autonomous, and deployable manipulation systems. We design and build advanced robotic platforms ranging from lightweight robotic arms to mobile dual-arm systems, targeting manipulation tasks in unstructured and contact-rich environments.

Our research philosophy emphasizes the tight integration of model-based methods and learning-driven techniques. This includes physics-based modeling and control, data-driven and learning-based manipulation, as well as emerging vision–language–action (VLA) and multimodal perception frameworks. By combining mechanical design, sensing, control, and AI, we aim to bridge the gap between laboratory research and practical robotic deployment.

Open Positions

We welcome applications from:

  • Robotics engineers
  • Research assistants
  • Research interns

Candidates with interests or experience in the following areas are especially encouraged to apply:

  • Robotic manipulation and control
  • Mobile manipulation and dual-arm systems
  • Vision-based and multimodal perception
  • Learning-based control, imitation learning, and reinforcement learning
  • System integration and real-world experimentation

  • Opportunities to work on cutting-edge research projects with real-world impact
  • A collaborative research environment combining theory, algorithms, and hardware
  • Strong support for academic publications and technology transfer

If you are interested in joining us or would like to learn more, please feel free to reach out or follow our future updates on this blog.
